Why this reads differently than Aerotrack
  • Simulation and trade-study first, not an ML product shell.
  • Dynamic tasking, heterogeneous fleets, and policy evaluation instead of API-driven inference.
  • Closer to operations analysis, data science, autonomy evaluation, and data engineering workstreams.
  • Built around explainable mission tradeoffs you can walk through in an interview.

Mission snapshot
